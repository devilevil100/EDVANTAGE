How to be a Tutor



With a 12th grade passing certification, and little bit of communication skills and loads of love for children and doing good for the society, you can apply to teach in our centres too. The childrens safety is of utmost importance at all times, and because of that we need to be absolutely sure that you are the right fit for being a role model for these kids. After filling up the online form after downloading our app/logging in through our website and providing all documents neccessary to deem you fit to teach, we need to interview you on a one on one basis to remove any and all doubts of you not being good for a teachers position. When that's done, all you have to do is book a time slot whenever you are free and in the mood of introducing the chilren to a world of precious knowlegde currently limited to a minority of the population and share.

How do we work

Registration

You can either join in either as a student or as a tutor. As a student you just need to enter your email and location in the form so that we can inform you about centres and classes. To register as a tutor you need to provide your aadhar card, degree certificate and resume. After that our team would verify the details for security of our students and then you can access the whole website.

Challenges

The biggest problem today is about Covid. As we know that there can be a third wave anytime soon, so we need to take precautions for the same. We are providing sanitized centres, n95 masks and face shield to all our students for the same.

Why choose us

We aim to provide education to all students in india. Most of the students these days are having their school classes online, but we all know that there is a major difference between online and offline teaching. students can ask all their doubts without any worries about network connections in their nearest centres.
